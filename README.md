## About The Project

Course Selection: Helping students pick courses based on their major, interests, or prerequisites.
Schedule Management: Assisting students in creating a balanced course schedule.
Career Planning: Offering advice on courses that align with career goals or further education.

## Usage

use the system to create and manage their academic profiles, explore the course catalog, and build their degree plans. They can search for and view course details, construct their schedules using a drag-and-drop interface, and track their academic progress through a visual dashboard. Personalized recommendations help them select courses aligned with their goals. Alerts and reminders keep them informed about deadlines and prerequisites, while advising notes from their advisors offer additional guidance.
